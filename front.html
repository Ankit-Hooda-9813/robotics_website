About

Welcome to the Robotics Club of IIT JAMMU, where innovation, collaboration, and passion converge to create a world of possibilities! Our club is dedicated to nurturing a community of enthusiastic minds interested in the fascinating realm of robotics and automation. Together with IIT JAMMU, we work on exciting projects and pushing the boundaries of what robotics can achieve. At the heart of our club lies a relentless pursuit of knowledge and understanding. Guided by a dedicated team of mentors and experienced robotics enthusiasts, the club's workshops and seminars are tailored to cater to all levels of proficiency. The Robotics Club emphasizes the importance of fostering leadership, teamwork, and effective communication. Members are encouraged to take on leadership roles, organizing events, and guiding project teams to success. As a community, we are not confined to the walls of our campus. The Robotics Club actively engages with the wider community, organizing outreach programs and workshops. After years of experiences we have polished our skills and due to that we have won many Inter IIT and national level competitions. Throughout the academic year, the Robotics Club participates in numerous inter and intra college competitions, where members put their knowledge and skills to the test. Through exciting projects we gain and test our passion for robotics. The Robotics Club is more than just a club, it's a family united by a passion for robotics. Members forge deep into ever lasting friendships and meeting like minded people. These experiences also help a person to grow beyond their academic journey. Join us as we dive into the world of coding, electronics, and design. Come and be a part of the robotics revolution!
